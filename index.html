About Cyber Security

Cyber security involves protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. It includes practices and technologies to safeguard information integrity, confidentiality, and availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a cyber attack?

A cyber attack is any attempt to expose, alter, disable, destroy, steal, or gain unauthorized access to or make unauthorized use of an asset.

How can I protect my passwords?

Use strong passwords that include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using easily guessable information and consider using a password manager.
